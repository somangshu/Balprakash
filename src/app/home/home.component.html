Bal Prakash Trust Ajmer

The Dream
Bal Prakash, a tangible dream, nestles in the now greened undulating land stretching over eleven bighas off Ajmer by-pass Beawar Road near Village- Badgaon, Ajmer, Rajasthan. Founded in 1996 by Shri Tikaram Paliwal & Prakash Paliwal Janhitkari Trust, it seeks to insure the child's today to ensure a meaningful tomorrow.

The Vision & Mission

Life at Bal Prakash is geared to inculcating self-reliance. Activities that sustain the ties of these rural children with the soil are encouraged and an endeavour is made to engage them in elementary horticulture, vermiculture, cattle keeping for dairy, organic farming, nursery practices, carpentry, tailoring, making cloth/paper bags and sundry items of everyday use made from waste material, albeit at an elementary level, are afoot.
